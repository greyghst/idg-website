Who We Are

A Bridge Between
World Producers
and U.S. Consumers

Irongate Distribution Group is a premier import and distribution company founded on a singular vision: to connect the world's most compelling beverage producers and consumer goods brands with the opportunities and consumers the U.S. market offers.

We operate at the intersection of global trade expertise and deep U.S. market knowledge — navigating complex regulatory landscapes, building distributor relationships, and crafting market strategies that turn international brands into household names.

From discovery and sourcing through compliance, logistics, and retail placement, IDG is the full-service partner that international producers trust to represent their brands with excellence on American soil.

We source from producing regions across Europe, the Americas, Asia, and beyond — with established relationships that grant access to exceptional products before they reach the open market.
02
U.S. Market Mastery

Brand Integrity
Every brand we represent is carefully vetted and genuinely supported. We are invested partners in your success, not just a logistics conduit.

好結果 · Artisan Fruit Snacks
Taiwan · Kaohsiung

Goodie Fruitie (好結果) is a Taiwanese artisan brand built around a singular idea: celebrating Taiwan's extraordinary fruit heritage in its most pure and beautiful form. Founded by Yi-Ming Agriculture & Biotech Farm, the brand transforms fresh-harvested Taiwanese fruits into premium dried and specialty products with no artificial additives — just fruit, care, and craft.

A standout in the lineup is the Miracle Berry (神秘果) product, a rare West African fruit long cultivated in Taiwan that temporarily transforms sour flavors into sweet. Beautifully packaged for gifting, Goodie Fruitie occupies a premium food-as-experience niche that resonates deeply with U.S. specialty retail and wellness-conscious consumers.

Redbloom
Gut Healthy Chili Crisp
United States

Redbloom is a first-of-its-kind gut-healthy chili crisp, clinically formulated to let spice lovers enjoy bold flavor without digestive discomfort. Organic chilis are microencapsulated in avocado oil — delivering authentic heat while protecting gut lining — making Redbloom the first chili crisp backed by clinical dosages and capsaicin therapy protocols for IBS relief.

As seen in Men's Health and Eater, and endorsed by gastroenterologists from Stanford Medicine and UChicago, Redbloom is HSA/FSA eligible — a rare distinction for a food product. Available in three heat levels (Umami, Aroma, Dopamine), it's no-seed-oil, low FODMAP, gluten-free, and vegan.

OMAR
Taiwanese Whisky
Taiwan · Nantou Distillery

OMAR is Taiwan's most celebrated single malt whisky, produced at the Nantou Distillery nestled in the mountains of central Taiwan. Crafted by Taiwan Tobacco & Liquor Corporation, OMAR — meaning 'amber' in Gaelic — is distinguished by its tropical fruity character, born from the island's unique subtropical climate and pristine mountain water.

Non-chill filtered and free of caramel coloring, OMAR presents natural terroir in every bottle. Award-winning at ISC, SFWSC, IWSC, WWA, and CMB, including Supreme Winner and Grand Gold Medal. OMAR has earned its place on the world whisky stage.
